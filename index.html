Courses We Offer

Lets Dive Into Them

Intermediate

The Pre-University Course or Pre-Degree Course (PUC or PDC) is an Intermediate Course (which is known as 10+2) of two years' duration, refers to Class 11th and Class 12th and called as 1st PUC and 2nd PUC respectively in the PU Colleges or Junior Colleges and are conducted by state education institutions

Degree

A Degree Is actually a 3 to 4 Years Course conferred by a UGC-recognised university, after the completion of a course.

Post Graduation

A postgraduate course is typically one year’s duration full time or it can take two years to complete part-time. A postgraduate diploma, postgraduate degree or Master's degree offers the student an advanced level of education in their chosen area of study.
